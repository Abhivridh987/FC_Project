Kumbalangi is a charming village in Kerala, India, known for its serene backwaters, traditional fishing techniques, and sustainable tourism model. It is the first model tourism village in India, offering visitors an authentic experience of Kerala’s rural life. The village is famous for its Chinese fishing nets, lush mangroves, and picturesque landscapes. Visitors can explore local culture through canoe rides, village walks, and engaging with the friendly fishing community. Kumbalangi is a perfect getaway for those seeking a peaceful and immersive experience in the heart of Kerala’s backwaters.
