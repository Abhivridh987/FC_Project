Wayanad, located in the Western Ghats of Kerala, is a stunning hill district known for its lush green forests, misty mountains, and rich biodiversity. It is home to numerous wildlife sanctuaries, waterfalls, and caves, making it a haven for adventure enthusiasts and nature lovers. The Edakkal Caves, with ancient petroglyphs, and the scenic Banasura Sagar Dam are major attractions. Wayanad offers thrilling trekking routes to places like Chembra Peak and Meenmutty Waterfalls. The district is also famous for its spice plantations and indigenous tribal culture, providing visitors with a unique and immersive experience.
