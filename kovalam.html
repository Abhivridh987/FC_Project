Kovalam is one of the most famous beach destinations in Kerala, known for its serene beaches, shallow waters, and vibrant nightlife. It features three beautiful crescent-shaped beaches: Lighthouse Beach, Hawa Beach, and Samudra Beach. The iconic lighthouse at Lighthouse Beach offers breathtaking panoramic views of the Arabian Sea. Kovalam is also a hub for Ayurvedic wellness centers, offering rejuvenating therapies. With water sports like surfing, catamaran rides, and parasailing, Kovalam provides an unforgettable seaside experience for visitors looking for relaxation and adventure alike.
