Jadayu Para, also known as Jadayu Earth’s Center, is a unique tourist destination in Kerala, India. It is famous for the world’s largest bird sculpture, depicting Jatayu from the Indian epic Ramayana. The site is known for its breathtaking views, adventure activities, and cultural significance. Visitors can experience rock climbing, zip-lining, a ropeway ride to the top, and a virtual reality museum that narrates the legend of Jatayu. Surrounded by lush greenery and hills, Jatayu Para is a perfect blend of mythological history and modern adventure tourism, making it a must-visit destination.
