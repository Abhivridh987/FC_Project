Payyoli, a scenic coastal town in Kerala, is known for its golden sandy beaches, shallow waters, and calm waves. The beach is a favorite spot for relaxation and water sports. It is also famous as a nesting site for the endangered Olive Ridley turtles, attracting nature lovers and conservationists.
