Bekal is a historic town in Kerala known for its majestic Bekal Fort, the largest fort in the state, which overlooks the Arabian Sea. The fort offers panoramic views of the coastline, making it a favorite spot for photographers and history enthusiasts. Bekal Beach, with its golden sands and serene atmosphere, is perfect for evening strolls and relaxation. The town is rich in Malabar culture and cuisine, offering visitors an authentic Kerala experience. With its mix of history, nature, and coastal beauty, Bekal is an ideal destination for travelers seeking a peaceful yet captivating getaway.
