Kumarakom, a beautiful backwater destination in Kerala, is known for its tranquil waters, lush paddy fields, and abundant birdlife. Situated on the banks of Vembanad Lake, it offers stunning views and a serene escape into nature. The Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary attracts bird watchers with migratory birds like Siberian cranes and herons.
