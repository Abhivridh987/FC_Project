Kubaba Kuzhi Mandi in Kochi is a popular spot offering a fusion of Arabic and Malabar flavors. Famous for its Kuzhi Mandi, a traditional dish made with aromatic rice and slow-cooked meat, Kubaba is a must-visit for anyone looking to savor authentic Middle Eastern-inspired Kerala cuisine. The Mandi is perfectly paired with rich spices and served with grilled chicken or lamb, making it a favorite among locals and tourists alike. With its inviting ambiance and mouth-watering menu, Kubaba is a place you won't want to miss during your visit to Kochi.
