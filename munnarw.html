Munnar is a picturesque hill station located in the Western Ghats of Kerala, India. Known for its lush green tea plantations, mist-covered mountains, and tranquil atmosphere, it is a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Munnar is also home to rich biodiversity, with wildlife sanctuaries like Eravikulam National Park, which is famous for its Nilgiri Tahr population. The cool climate, scenic viewpoints, and serene landscapes make it an ideal getaway for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life. Additionally, Munnar offers opportunities for trekking, boating, and exploring local culture.
